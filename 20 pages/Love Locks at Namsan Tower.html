Love Locks at Namsan Tower

You aren't required to put up any love locks anywhere but do head up to Namsan Tower to get the best view of the surrounding area. Known as N Seoul Tower or Seoul Tower, the view here is possibly one of the best. It's a popular attraction in South Korea for those with romantic relationships.
