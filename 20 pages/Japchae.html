Japchae / Stir-Fried Glass Noodles

The famous glass noodles are another must-try for anyone visiting Korea. This is arguably one of the most popular noodle dishes in the country. These glass noodles are typically made from sweet potatoes. Then, the noodles are stirred in sesame oil, mushrooms, assorted vegetables, and beef.
